About Us

Renowned for its expertise in Ecommerce and social commerce-oriented logistics.

Founded in 2012, we recognized early on that seamless logistics are crucial for Ecommerce and social commerce to thrive. Our mission began with simplifying last-mile deliveries, making logistics accessible for individuals and corporations alike, anywhere, and anytime.

Since our inaugural shipment in 2012, GDS has rapidly expanded, linking African vendors with global buyers and sellers through our streamlined "overseas shipping services" spanning multiple continents.

At GDS, meticulous attention to detail guides every aspect, from packaging and handling to the final delivery to end-users. Our agility, dedication to service excellence, and data-driven approach enable us to offer enduring solutions to complex logistical challenges. Upholding our corporate, environmental, and social responsibilities, we adhere strictly to ethical business practices.

Leveraging extensive local knowledge, GDS combines scale and experience to consistently deliver superior outcomes. Hundreds of thousands of verified users and ecosystem partners, including top businesses, rely on GDS for secure shipments within and beyond Africa.

Continually innovating, GDS explores new avenues and groundbreaking concepts to optimize and bridge the gap between Ecommerce, social commerce, and logistics.

With a robust growth trajectory, GDS Logistics has established a strong presence throughout Nigeria and operates across Ghana and three additional continents.

Today, we are shaping the future of logistics.
